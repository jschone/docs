<Info>
  **What's a helper?** A helper is your personal tracking tool that captures and organizes anything you want to remember or monitor-expenses, workouts, medications, ideas, and more.
</Info>

## Create Your Account

Before you can create helpers, you'll need a Peanuts account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to peanutsapp.com">
    Visit [peanutsapp.com](https://peanutsapp.com) in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Get Started'">
    Click the **Get Started** button in the navigation bar or hero section.

    <Frame>
      ![Get Started button on landing page](/images/quickstart/get-started-button.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign up with email">
    Enter your email address and create a password. You'll receive a confirmation email-click the link to verify your account.

    <Tip>
      You can also sign in with Google for faster access.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

        **Example prompts:**

        - "Track my daily water intake"
        - "Log my expenses"

## Add Your First Entry

Now let's log something to your new helper!

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your helper">
    From the home screen, click on your helper card to open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the fields">
    Enter your data in the provided fields. Required fields are marked with a red indicator.

    <Frame>
      ![Entry form with fields](/images/quickstart/add-entry-form.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your entry">
    Click the **Add** or **Log** button (depending on your helper type). You'll see a success message confirming your entry was saved.

    <Check>
      Your entry is now recorded with a timestamp and appears in your history.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Pro tip:** Use voice entry! Click the microphone icon to speak your entry instead of typing. Peanuts will transcribe and parse your speech automatically.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My helper didn't generate correctly">
    Try being more specific in your description. Instead of "track stuff," say "track my daily water intake in glasses."

    You can also refine your helper after creation using the **Tweak** button or natural language chat.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I can't create more helpers">
    Free accounts are limited to 3 active helpers. You can either:

    - Delete an existing helper to make room
    - Pause helpers you're not actively using
    - [Upgrade to a paid plan](/pricing) for higher limits
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My entry didn't save">
    Check that all required fields are filled in (marked with red). If using voice entry, ensure your microphone permissions are enabled.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The AI asked me clarifying questions">
    This is normal! When your request could be interpreted multiple ways, Peanuts asks follow-up questions to create the best helper for your needs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
