## AI Credits System

Credits are the currency for AI-powered features in Peanuts. Different operations consume different amounts of credits based on their complexity.

### Credit Costs

| Operation | Credits | Examples |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| **Text AI** | 1 | AI chat, helper creation, smart nudges |
| **Vision AI** | 5 | Receipt scanning, smart scan, image analysis |
| **Speech-to-Text** | 10 | Voice entry, hands-free mode |
| **Text-to-Speech** | 15 | Voice readback, audio responses |

### Credit Reset Cycle

<Note>
  Credits reset **monthly** on your billing date. Unused credits do **not** roll over to the next month.
</Note>

### Monitoring Your Credits

Track your credit usage in two ways:

1. **Header Counter** â€” Quick glance at remaining credits from any page
2. **Credits Usage Dashboard** â€” Detailed breakdown at `/settings/credits`

The dashboard shows:
- Remaining credits and usage percentage
- Breakdown by operation type (pie chart)
- Recent activity (bar chart)
- Full transaction history

## Managing Your Subscription

### Upgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to **Settings â†’ Billing**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Change Plan">
    Select the **Change Plan** button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose New Tier">
    Select Starter, Pro, or Team
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Billing Period">
    Choose monthly or yearly billing
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Enter payment details and confirm
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Upgrades take effect **immediately**. You'll receive the new credit allocation right away.
</Tip>

### Changing Billing Period

Switch between monthly and yearly billing from the Billing page. When switching to yearly:
- Pay for 10 months upfront
- Get 2 months free
- New billing cycle starts immediately

### Cancelling Your Subscription

<Warning>
  Cancelling your subscription will downgrade you to the **Free** tier at the end of your current billing period.
</Warning>

**What you'll lose:**
- Access to premium features (Voice, Try-On, Ask Peanuts)
- Credit allocation above 50/month
- Helpers beyond the 3-helper limit (archived, not deleted)
- Data history beyond 7 days
- Telegram and webhook integrations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to **Settings â†’ Billing**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Cancel Subscription">
    Find the cancel option in your plan card
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Cancellation">
    Review what you'll lose and confirm
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your data is **never deleted** when you cancel. Archived helpers and entries remain accessible if you resubscribe.
</Note>

## Low Credit Alerts

Peanuts sends email notifications when your credits are running low to help you avoid interruptions.

### Alert Thresholds

You'll receive an email when credits drop to:
- **10 credits** remaining
- **5 credits** remaining  
- **3 credits** remaining
- **1 credit** remaining

### Opting Out

If you prefer not to receive these notifications:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Notifications">
    Scroll to the **Notifications** section
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle Off">
    Disable **Low-credit email alerts**
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When do my credits reset?">
    Credits reset on your monthly billing date. For example, if you subscribed on the 15th, credits reset on the 15th of each month.
    
    You can see your next reset date in the **Credits Usage** dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens to unused credits?">
    Unused monthly credits do **not** roll over. They reset to your plan's allocation on your billing date.
    
    However, **purchased credits** (from top-ups) never expire and remain until used.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Contact support within 7 days of purchase for subscription refunds.
    
    Credit pack purchases are generally non-refundable once credits have been used.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do purchased credits expire?">
    No! Credits purchased through top-up packs **never expire**. They remain in your account until used, even if you cancel your subscription.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What payment methods are accepted?">
    Peanuts accepts:
    - Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
    - Apple Pay
    - Google Pay
    - iDEAL (Netherlands)
    - Bancontact (Belgium)
    - And more regional methods
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is my payment information secure?">
    Absolutely. Peanuts uses **Adyen**, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. Your card details are never stored on our servers.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I pause my subscription?">
    Currently, pausing isn't available. You can cancel and resubscribe when ready â€” your data will be preserved.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if a payment fails?">
    You'll receive an email notification. Your subscription remains active for a short grace period while you update your payment method.
    
    Update your card in **Settings â†’ Billing** to avoid service interruption.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
