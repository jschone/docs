---
title: "Welcome to Peanuts"
description: "Create tiny, single-purpose apps that live in your messaging apps"
---

Peanuts lets you create very small apps that do one thing. Track a habit. Collect ideas. Build a checklist. Remember the details. 

They live in your pocket, accessible from Telegram or the web, and disappear when you don't need them.
