## Quick Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings** from the main menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your timezone">
    Select your timezone so reminders arrive at the right local time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure digests">
    Choose when to receive your morning and evening summaries.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Quiet Hours">
    Block notifications during sleep or focus time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Daily Digests

Get a summary of your tracked data delivered at times you choose.

| Digest | Default Time | Content |
|--------|--------------|---------|
| Morning | 8:00 AM | Yesterday's summary, today's goals |
| Evening | 8:00 PM | Today's progress, pending items |

<Tip>
Digests are sent via your connected channels - Telegram, email, or both.
</Tip>

### Customizing Digest Time

1. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Notification Schedule**
2. Tap the time next to Morning or Evening digest
3. Select your preferred time
4. Changes apply immediately

## Quiet Hours

Block all notifications during specific hours.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Quiet Hours">
    Toggle on **Quiet Hours** in Settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set start time">
    Choose when quiet mode begins (e.g., 10:00 PM).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set end time">
    Choose when notifications resume (e.g., 8:00 AM).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Quiet Hours apply across all channels - in-app, Telegram, and email digests are all silenced.
</Note>

## Per-Helper Reminders

Each helper can have its own notification settings.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Daily Reminders" icon="clock">
Get a nudge at a specific time to log your data.

1. Open any helper
2. Tap **Settings** (gear icon)
3. Enable **Daily Reminder**
4. Set your preferred time

Great for habits, medications, or daily check-ins.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No-Entry Alerts" icon="triangle-exclamation">
Get notified if you forget to log data for a helper.

1. Open helper settings
2. Enable **No-Entry Alert**
3. Choose the alert time

Peanuts checks if you've logged anything that day - if not, you get a gentle reminder.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Low-Credit Alerts

<Info>
Receive email alerts when your AI credits are running low.
</Info>

Alerts trigger at these thresholds:
- 10 credits remaining
- 5 credits remaining
- 3 credits remaining
- 1 credit remaining

### Disabling Low-Credit Emails

1. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Notifications**
2. Toggle off **Low-credit email alerts**

## Channel Configuration

### Telegram

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your bot">
    Set up your Telegram bot in **Settings** â†’ **Telegram**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable notifications">
    Toggle on digest and reminder notifications.
  </Step>

<Steps>

  <Step title="Enable email digest">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose frequency">
    Select daily or weekly digest delivery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Not receiving notifications">
    <div>
      <ul>
        <li>Check your timezone is set correctly</li>
        <li>Verify Quiet Hours aren&apos;t active</li>
        <li>Ensure the channel (Telegram/email) is connected</li>
        <li>Check spam/junk folders for emails</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Wrong notification times">
    <div>
      <ul>
        <li>Update your timezone in Settings</li>
        <li>Reminder times display in your local timezone</li>
        <li>Changes may take a few minutes to apply</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Too many notifications">
    <div>
      <ul>
        <li>Disable per-helper reminders you don&apos;t need</li>
        <li>Turn off Smart Nudges in helper settings</li>
        <li>Enable Quiet Hours for focus time</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Telegram notifications not working">
    <div>
      <ul>
        <li>Send <code>/start</code> to your bot to re-authorize</li>
        <li>Check that notifications are enabled in Telegram app settings</li>
        <li>Verify the bot is still connected in Peanuts settings</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy

<Note>
Notification content is personalized but never shared. Your data stays private.
</Note>

- Notifications are stored only for your account
- Telegram messages are end-to-end with your bot
- Email digests are sent only to your verified address
- You can delete all notification history in Account settings
