## How Try-On Works

### The Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload base photo">
    Provide a clear photo of yourself or your space. This becomes the canvas for try-ons.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select catalog item">
    Tap an item from your catalog that you want to visualize.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI generates preview">
    Gemini Vision composites the item onto your base photo, creating a realistic preview.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & save">
    View the result. Save it, download it, or try again for a different variation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Behind the Scenes

| Component | Technology |
|-----------|------------|
| **Image analysis** | Gemini Vision AI |
| **Compositing** | AI-powered blending |
| **Storage** | Secure cloud storage |
| **Sharing** | Signed URLs (7-day expiry) |

<Note>
Try-on results are saved to your account and accessible for 7 days via shareable links. Download results you want to keep permanently.
</Note>

## Base Photo Guidelines

### For Best Results

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Do" icon="check">
    - Use good lighting
    - Face camera directly
    - Wear fitted clothing
    - Use neutral background
    - High resolution photos
  </Card>
  <Card title="Avoid" icon="xmark">
    - Dark or shadowy photos
    - Busy backgrounds
    - Extreme angles
    - Low resolution images
    - Motion blur
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### By Visualizer Type

| Type | Base Photo Tips |
|------|-----------------|
| **Wardrobe** | Full body, front-facing, arms slightly away |
| **Makeup** | Close-up face, good lighting, no glasses |
| **Hair** | Face visible, current hair showing |
| **Furniture** | Room photo, clear floor/wall space |
| **Car colors** | Side view, good lighting, clean background |

## Tips & Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use consistent base photos" icon="image">
    For fair comparisons, use the same base photo when trying multiple items. Different lighting or angles make comparison harder.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Organize by category" icon="folder-tree">
    As your catalog grows, categories keep things manageable. Set up categories before adding lots of items.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Save favorites immediately" icon="heart">
    Like a result? Save it right away. You can always delete later, but regenerating the exact same result isn't guaranteed.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Download for permanence" icon="download">
    Sharing links expire. Download results you want to keep forever or reference later.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Try multiple times" icon="rotate">
    AI generation has variability. If the first result isn't perfect, try again‚Äîyou might get a better composite.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
