## How Templates Work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe Your Need">
    Tell Peanuts what you want to track or organize in plain language.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Selects Template">
    The generator picks the best template based on your description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fields Auto-Configure">
    Relevant fields, widgets, and rules are pre-populated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Theme Applied">
    Colors and icons match your domain (health, finance, hobbies, etc.).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
You never have to choose a template manually—just describe what you need and Peanuts handles the rest.
</Tip>

## Customization

Every template supports:

### Fields
Add, remove, or modify data fields:
- Text, Number, Date, Time
- Select dropdowns, Checkboxes
- Images, Smart Scan (OCR)
- Calculated fields

### Widgets
Add visual components:
- Charts (Area, Bar, Line, Pie)
- Counters and Progress bars
- Streaks and Ratings
- Timers and Stopwatches

### Rules
Enforce data quality:
- Required fields
- Min/max values
- "Held" states for incomplete entries

<Note>
Customization happens in **Edit Mode**—tap the pencil icon on any helper.
</Note>

## Choosing the Right Template

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I want to log something daily">
    Use **Tracker** for flexible data or **Habit** for simple yes/no completion.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I have a repeating checklist">
    Use **Checklist**—it resets automatically (daily, weekly, or on-demand).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I'm tracking events or appointments">
    Use **Schedule**—entries are anchored to dates with calendar export.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I want to collect things over time">
    Use **List** for simple collections or **Visualizer** for items you want to preview/try on.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I'm working toward a goal">
    Use **Plan** for milestone-based progress or **Habit** for daily consistency.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I need structured data capture">
    Use **Form** for inspections, intake, or any multi-field submissions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
