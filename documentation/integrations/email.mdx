## Quick Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    Ensure your account email is verified. Check for a verification email or resend from **Settings → Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Digests">
    Go to **Settings → Preferences** and toggle on **Email Digests**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Frequency">
    Select **Daily**, **Weekly**, or both.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done">
    Your first digest arrives at your chosen time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Daily Digests

A morning email summarizing what you logged yesterday.

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Time** | Delivered at 8:00 AM in your timezone |
| **Content** | Entry counts, streaks, key stats per helper |
| **Format** | Clean HTML email with quick links |

<Tip>
Daily digests are perfect for building reflection habits—review yesterday before starting today.
</Tip>

---

## Weekly Recaps

A comprehensive summary delivered every **Monday morning**.

**Includes:**
- Total entries across all helpers
- Streak progress and milestones
- Week-over-week comparisons
- Highlights and patterns

<Info>
Weekly recaps aggregate data from your Global Digest setting. Enable it in **Settings → Preferences**.
</Info>

## Per-Helper Digests

Each helper can have its own email schedule:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Helper Settings">
    Navigate to your helper and tap the **Settings** (gear) icon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Notifications">
    Scroll to the **Notifications** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Email">
    Enable email notifications and set the frequency.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Forwarding to Others

Share a helper's activity with someone else:

1. In helper settings, find **Forward Digest To**
2. Enter their email address
3. They'll receive the same digest you do

<Note>
Forwarding is great for coaches, partners, or managers who need visibility without app access.
</Note>

## Accountability Partners

Set up an **Accountability Partner** to receive your progress:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What they receive">
    - Weekly PDF reports with stats and charts
    - Entry summaries (no private notes unless you include them)
    - Streak and consistency metrics
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How to configure">
    1. Open your helper's **Settings**
    2. Scroll to **Reports**
    3. Enable **Scheduled Reports**
    4. Enter their email in **Send To**
    5. Choose frequency (Weekly recommended)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Privacy controls">
    - You choose which helpers share data
    - Accountability partners cannot access your account
    - Stop sharing anytime by removing their email
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Managing Preferences

All email settings in one place:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Preferences**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Email Section">
    Scroll to **Email Notifications**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle Options">
    - **Email Digests** — Master toggle for digest emails
    - **Digest Day** — Choose which day for weekly recaps
    - **Low-Credit Alerts** — Notify when credits run low
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Emails not arriving">
    **Check:**
    - Your email is verified (Settings → Account)
    - Digests are enabled (Settings → Preferences)
    - Check spam/junk folder
    - Add `welcome@peanutsapp.com` to your contacts
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Wrong timezone">
    Digests use your account timezone. Update it in **Settings → Preferences → Timezone**.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Too many emails">
    Disable per-helper digests if you only want the global summary. Or set helpers to forward instead of duplicate.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Accountability partner not receiving">
    - Verify the email address is correct
    - Ask them to check spam folder
    - Ensure scheduled reports are enabled for that helper
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
