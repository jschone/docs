## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Number">
    Link your WhatsApp Business account through the Meta Business Suite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Webhook">
    Peanuts provides a webhook URL to receive your messages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Messaging">
    Send texts, photos, or voice notes to log entries to your helpers.
  </Step>
</Steps>
