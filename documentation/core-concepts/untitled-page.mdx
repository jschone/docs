Templates are pre-configured Helpers that you can clone and customize. They provide a fast way to get started without describing everything from scratch.

## How Templates Work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse the Gallery">
    Explore templates organized by category.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview the Structure">
    See what fields, widgets, and settings are included.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clone to Your Account">
    One click creates your own copy of the template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize (Optional)">
    Modify fields, add widgets, or adjust settings using natural language.
  </Step>
</Steps>
